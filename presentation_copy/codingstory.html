My Coding Story
by Angelina Chu

Hi! I'm Angie. As you may know from my previous projects, I LOVE to bake.

However, I often have found that I really only present the final products of baking...

Although fun nevertheless, the process of baking can be quite unorganized and chaotic sometimes. I wanted to make a website about my baking process not only as a functional tool to keep myself more organized while baking, but also as a way to share how I like to baje.

As I bake with an emphasis on not only a cake's flavor but also its decoration, I decided on four categories to focus on in my cake making process.

1. Recipes
I normally struggle with scouering my internet search history to find some of my favorite recipes because I had never kept track of them before. Also, as I have started sharing my treats with friends and family, sometimes I get asked about the recipes I use and often struggle to track down the one that I used.

2. Piping Tips
Piping has become one of my favorite parts of making cakes because of the artistic possibilities it presents. However, I have collected so many random ones over the years that I can often get confused about how each one pipes from just looking at them.

3. Tools
Just like piping tips (one of the tools used), I have developed a roster of favorite tools I use to make each and every cake. I started out with barely using any of these, but they have become essentials in baking for me and I can't imagine baking without them!

4. Gallery A way to look back on my past work!
